Satellite Insights - by Around the World

From a a handful of satellites in year X to over 9000 today - space has never been more crowded...

Curious to know more the reason behind the satellite boom?

The explosion in the number of satellites is the direct result of two interconnected forces: an ownership shift from government to commercial entities focused on mass-manufactured, short-lifespan Low Earth Orbit (LEO) satellites, and the radical reduction in launch costs enabled by reusable rockets, exemplified by the market dominance of SpaceX's Falcon 9.

But too many satellites is making the lower orbits more congested...
